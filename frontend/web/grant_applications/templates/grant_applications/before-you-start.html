What you will need

To complete the application you will need the following information to hand:

  • details of the event you are interested in exhibiting at
  • details of your business and the products or services it provides
  • the business turnover for the last 3 fiscal years

In addition you will also need to provide details of:

  • the event you are interested in exhibiting at
  • any additional financial contributions to your costs of exhibiting at the event
  • any State Aid received over the last 3 years
  • any DIT trade advisors or Trade Challenge Partners (TCPs) you are in contact with
  • any export experience you may have and your export strategy
  • any trade show experience you may have
