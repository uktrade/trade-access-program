Apply for a Tradeshow Access Programme (TAP) grant

Use this service to apply for a TAP grant.

These are available for eligible businesses taking part in the programme and can help towards the direct costs of attending TAP supported events to promote your business. Following the event participants will need to provide evidence these costs have been paid in order to then claim the grant.

Grant eligibility

To be eligible for a grant you must be either:

  • a small or medium-sized enterprise (SME) with less than 250 employees, and an annual turnover not exceeding €50 million or an annual balance sheet total not exceeding €43 million
  • a business or organisation with less than 250 employees and an annual turnover of between £83,000 and £5 million
  • a UK university, UK government-funded centre of higher or further learning or UK government-funded research organisation

In addition, you must:

  • be based in the UK (excluding Isle of Man or the Channel Islands unless UK registered)
  • be within the State Aid limit of €200,000 over any rolling 3 year period
  • not receive any other contributions towards eligible costs which the grant you are applying for will offset in relation to participation at the TAP supported event
  • not be fully committed to attend the event prior to seeking the grant

The application takes around 45 to 60 minutes.
