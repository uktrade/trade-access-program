Apply for a Tradeshow Access Programme (TAP) grant

Use this service to apply for a TAP grant.

These grants help eligible businesses with the costs of attending TAP-supported events to promote your business in export markets. Following the event, participants need to provide evidence of these costs in order to claim the allocated grant.

Grant eligibility

To be eligible for a TAP grant you must be either:

  • a small or medium-sized enterprise (SME) with fewer than 250 employees, and an annual turnover not exceeding €50 million, or an annual balance sheet total not exceeding €43 million
  • a business or organisation with fewer than 250 employees and an annual turnover of between £83,000 and £5 million
  • a UK university, UK government-funded centre of higher or further learning, or UK government-funded research organisation

In addition, you must:

  • be based in the UK (excluding Isle of Man or the Channel Islands unless UK registered)
  • be within the State Aid limit of €200,000 over any rolling 3-year period
  • not receive any other contributions towards eligible costs the TAP grant will offset
  • not have booked your place at the event before applying for the TAP grant

The application takes around 45 to 60 minutes.
