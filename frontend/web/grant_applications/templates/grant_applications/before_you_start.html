To complete the application you’ll need:

  • details of the event you’re interested in exhibiting at
  • details of your business and the products or services it provides
  • the business turnover for the last 3 fiscal years

In addition you will also need to provide details of:

  • any state aid received in the last 3 years
  • your trade show experience
  • your export experience
  • your export strategy and export plan, if you have one
  • any DIT trade advisors or DIT-approved trade associations (also known as Trade Challenge Partners) that you’re in contact with
